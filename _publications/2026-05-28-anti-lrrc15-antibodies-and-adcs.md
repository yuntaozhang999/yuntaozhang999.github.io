These two related patents cover the development and application of anti-LRRC15 antibodies for cancer therapy. They represent a unified research effort yielding both the foundational antibodies and their specific application as drug conjugates.

*   **[WO/2026/112469](https://patentscope2.wipo.int/search/en/WO2026112469): ANTI-LRRC15 ANTIBODIES AND USES THEREOF.** Provides isolated anti-LRRC15 antibodies, including bispecific antibodies and ADCs, that specifically bind to and are internalized by cells expressing LRRC15. Includes methods for treating cancer and diagnostic uses.
*   **[WO/2026/112472](https://patentscope.wipo.int/search/en/WO2026112472): ANTI-LRRC15 ANTIBODY DRUG CONJUGATES.** Focuses specifically on ADCs where an anti-LRRC15 antibody is conjugated to exatecan via a linker. It details the chemical formulas of these conjugates and specific methods for cancer treatment by administering these ADCs.
